About Us
Who are we?
KA3 is a research company dedicated to using data and models that assist in investment decision making.
Why were we founded?
We were founded on the idea that there are no outfits out there that give retail investors both macro and micro views of the economy and the markets. Without them, there cannot be a holistic understanding of how they impact investment portfolios.
Our Mission
Our mission is to empower retail investors with tools that help them make better and informed decisions.
